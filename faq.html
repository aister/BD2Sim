How can I put two or more chassis into the sim?

Short answer is, you can't. Mixed chassis is more complicated, since there are prioritized targeting. And my brain cannot handle all the different variables that might and will happen in mixed chassis. So I've decided to ignore mixed chassis, for now.

How can I put avatar and unit's level?

Same answer to the above question. You can't. It is too complicated, and plus you cannot know the level of your enemy's avatar and unit anyway.

There are some inaccuracies in the sim

Yes. There are. Mostly to do with avatar and unit's level. But also the behaviour of cobra is still not really accurate. Currently the sim is assuming that:

  • The cobra will revive the first units that got damaged that round
  • The cobra will ignore original units count and might revive more units that was lost
  • All the cobra will be killed once all units are dead.

Fixing these problems proved to be difficult and complicated. So I, again, decided to ignore them.
